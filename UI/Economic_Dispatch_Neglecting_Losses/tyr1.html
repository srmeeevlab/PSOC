Theory

Purpose of the economic dispatch or optimal dispatch is to reduce the fuel costs in order to achieve minimum operating cost.

Purpose of the economic dispatch or optimal dispatch is to reduce the fuel costs in order to achieve minimum operating cost.

The economic dispatch problem is a sub problem of two different problems, i.e. Unit commitment and online dispatch.

There are two methods used to find the economic dispatch:

  • Base Load Method, where the most efficient unit is loaded to its maximum capability, then the second most efficient is loaded and so on.
  • Best Point Method (Incremental Method), where the units are successively loaded to their lowest heat rate point, beginning with the most efficient unit and working down to the least efficient unit.

The power balance constraint states that the total generation must meet the total demand (PD)
